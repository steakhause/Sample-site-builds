Glass Phoenix

Transformed and Reborn Through Fire.
Amorphous materials formed in fire
Melting then cooling to rigidity without crystallization.

Art

Expression through production.
Designs of appealing beauty.
Objects defined by aesthetic quality.

John Ryszka II

My goal is to create unique glass pieces that challenge the viewer to become a mindful observer. I hope to challenge the viewer to consciously focus their attention, to be cognizant of the world around them, and to ponder the scale of the universe and our place within it.
